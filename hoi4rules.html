HOI4 Ruleset

1. Diplomacy

a. Only allowed to justify on nations you have claims on and nations that should be in factions you are currently at war with. Japan can justify on Siam after fall of China.
 - See faction list at bottom
b. No boosting ideology or coups. Cannot invite nations that are not in the faction list below.
c. Allies have to accept Rhineland and Sudetenland. Soviets have to accept Tank Treaty. Germans have to do Molotov-Ribbentrop Pact before attacking Poland and abide by the pact when Poland capitulates. Romania has to give up Besserabia.
 - This is just the historical stuff


2. Strategic Bombing

a. When you put planes up on strategic bombing missions, you can't move them to a new airzone or change their missions for 2 weeks.
 - This is to prevent the whack-a-mole when someone using strategic bombers just changes the target airzone instantly when the defender puts planes up in defense, and both the defender and attacker have to keep the air menu open constantly moving and chasing the bombers.
b. Not allowed to specify bomber targets.
 - This is to prevent strategic bombers exclusively targeting things like airports and fuel silos which makes the defender unable to respond to the bombers.
c. Can't build strategic bombers, must use TAC.
 - Ryan says they're OP (and tbh he's not wrong)
